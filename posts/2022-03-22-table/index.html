Table表格

2022-03-22
HTML

表格

    <table border="1">
      <caption>人員名單</caption>
      <tr>                //table row
        <th>Name</th>     //table header 會顯示粗體字
        <th>Age</th>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td>Bill</td>     //table cell
        <td>28</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td>Amy</td>
        <td>16</td>
      </tr>
    </table>

合併儲存格

    <table border="1">
      <tr>
        <th>Name</th>
        <th colspan="2">Age</th>    //合併2格，垂直合併是rowspan
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td>Amy</td>
        <td>16</td>
        <td>girl</td>
      </tr>
    </table>
